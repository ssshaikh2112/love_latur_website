    It is a matter of pleasure and pride to be a part in development, growth and achievement of MIMSR Medical College, Latur, Maharashtra. The college was established under the Trust MAEER MIT World Peace Centre, Pune in 1990 with admission capacity of 150 students per year to MBBS course. Admissions are done through entrance test (NEET), Government of Maharashtra and central counseling at Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai. There are Postgraduate degree courses in the subjects of Medicine, Paedatircs, Dermatology and Venereology, Anesthesia, Orthopedics, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Ophthalmology, Surgery, ENT, Community Medicine, Pathology, Microbiology, Pharmacology, Physiology and Biochemistry Students are admitted as per rules of Government of Maharashtra. Campus is spread over 106 acres of land and fascinating land scape. There is beautiful blend of serenity of nature, service to patient and mankind and knowledge flow of medical sciences. College administration is student centric and keen to develop culture of value based education and to reach the position of excellence. Students and staff enjoy rich learning and teaching experience.
